WORK

Imagem Feminina embodies a strong commitment to competence, ensuring the expertise needed to deliver top-tier services. This beauty salon is dedicated to enhancing natural beauty, valuing each client’s uniqueness and comfort. Respect defines the approach, creating an inclusive sanctuary where every woman is celebrated. Integrity is the main bedrock, upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in every interaction. Imagem Feminina encapsulates all these values and more, providing a trusted haven for women’s empowerment and self-expression.

By combining the initials “I“ and “F“, representing “Imagem Feminina“ we have crafted a modular design for the entire brand. This symbol, generated through a pattern method, is not only the brand’s acronym, but also represents adaptability and creativity. The playful arrangement of the letters within a geometric shape speaks to the versatility and dynamic nature of the beauty salon, allowing for endless interpretations and recting the multifaceted beauty that Imagem Feminina embraces. It’s a vibrant and visually appealing representation of the brand’s values and ethos.
