LX TEAM is a sports group focused on teaching tennis and padel, offering tailored solutions for different audiences. With a commitment to quality, their programs range from beginner lessons to high-performance training. LX TEAM manages 10 school partnerships, 3 tennis schools, 1 padel school, and a competition group, supporting over 500 regular players. Their mission is to make both sports accessible to all ages, promoting physical well-being and social engagement through dynamic activities and comprehensive coaching.
