GP Open is a friendly tennis tournament held at Bom Sucesso, a place where friends and families gather every summer. Designed for a close community, the logo and t-shirts reflect the warmth and familiarity of this annual event. With a focus on unity and tradition, the GP Open brings a sense of togetherness to a tournament where everyone knows each other, celebrating the unique bond shared by those who vacation at Bom Sucesso.
