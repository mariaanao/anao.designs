WORK

At Daluci, creativity and innovation fuel passion for crafting exceptional jewelry. Emphasizing quality craftsmanship and meticulous attention to detail, every piece is meticulously created. Central to the philosophy is you, the essence of the designs, ensuring a personalized and customer-centric journey in the realm of custom-made jewelry.

In crafting the Daluci visual brand, the logo design was a thoughtful effort. The bold, strong lettering mirrored the boldness of the jewelry she creates, with a distinctive touch of replacing the ‘A’ with a triangle, a symbol close to her heart. Inside this, a subtly off-centered circle formed a hidden moon, adding mystique and intrigue. “Custom-made jewellery” below the logo clarified the brand’s focus for newcomers and shades of green within the circle acknowledged her previous design heritage and growth.
