Sorriso is a Portuguese coffee shop that launched its own brand of coffee, inspired by the rich local culture. The packaging features patterns inspired by traditional Portuguese tiles, known for their intricate and unique designs. These tiles reflect the essence of Portugal, adding a touch of heritage and beauty to every product. Sorriso’s brand combines quality coffee with the charm of Portuguese art and tradition.
