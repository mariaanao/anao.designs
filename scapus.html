Scapus is a Design Studio, focused on graphic design for events, either for other companies in the marketing area, or solo clients for occasions such as weddings, baptisms, etc. They also work for smaller companies who are looking for a rebrand, taking care of the logo, website, etc. They intend to give the clients a personalized experience where they can feel free to express all their ideas in order to create the best design possible to each and everyone of our clients.
