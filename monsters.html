WORK

This fanzine is a tribute to Jacqueline S. Casey, one of the most influential figures in graphic design. Inspired by her innovative approach and mastery of visual communication, this piece celebrates her groundbreaking work and lasting legacy. Each page not only honors her contributions but also serves as an experimental canvas, echoing the creativity and boldness that defined her career. This fanzine captures Casey’s ability to blend typography and design into striking visual narratives, offering a fresh perspective on her timeless influence in the design world.
